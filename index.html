Ashley Rhodes l CEO & PRESIDENT

Ashley Rhodes is a San Francisco native that started his� 
career as a Painting Contractor in 2000 as a partner at
Evans� & Rhodes Painting & Decorating. Ashley led many diverse projects to completion, including the Municipal Railway Maintenance Facility Building in 2003 and the Willie Mays� Boys & Girls Club in 2006. DEMO Rhodes l COO

DEMO Rhodes brings over 10 years of Project Management & Quality Control experience to DEMO Painting & Decorating. He began as an apprentice painter for the SFPUC, where he worked on the Water System Improvement Project (WSIP). After moving on from the SFPUC, DEMO became a junior contract compliance officer for the SFMTA, where he ensured contractors were complying with all workforce regulations. As COO, of DEMO Painting & Decorating, DEMO oversees all field operations, safety compliance and quality control efforts for projects. He serves as a direct resource for employees, always keeping job site safety as a first priority, morale high and quality output consistent.
